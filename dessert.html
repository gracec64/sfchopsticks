SweetHoney Dessert

Orginally originating from China. SweetHoney has made their debut across California with their Hong Kong desserts. They have locations located on Irving and in Colma.

  • Looking for an affordable but satisfying dessert? SweetHoney has got your back. A majority of their desserts are in the $5-$9 range and come in good proportions.

  • They not only offer desserts but also snacks like curly fries and drinks.

  • They use high quality natural sourced ingreidents that are avalible locally.

Stonemill Matcha

Head over to Stonemill Matcha for all your matcha cravings. From lattes to pastries to even salads. With curated matcha from Kyoto, Japan, they aim to serve the highest quality to thier customors.

  • Matcha has a lot of health beneifits, so with any item coponsufed you'll feel better in no time.

  • We reccomened the matcha cream puffs!

  • If you're not feeling the matcha then they also have japanese dishes like Sandos and Onigri.

Naya House

Perfect place to grab a nice cold refreshing dish on a hot summer day. They offer instagramable desserts that aren't common in San Francisco like bingsoo and crepe cake. Their atmoshpere feels like cafe and library mixed together.
  • Their variety is across Asia. From Korean desserts to Japanese to Thai desserts. They have it.

  • You get what you pay for, the proportions are decents and will defineity make you full.

  • If you been wanting some LadyM but can't drive all the way down to LA then you should try Naya's crepe cake, a perfect dupe.

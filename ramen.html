Marufuku Ramen

Although there are many ramen spots in Japantown, Marufuku Ramen is the best of them all. Located in the west mall, this place has a long line before it even opens. So make sure to join the wait-list over on yelp to avoid standing in line!

  • With 2 main type of ramen, you can't go wrong with choosing either.

  • Order the DX ramens for a larger portion.

  • You can order extra topping or noodles if what's given isn't enough.

Nojo Ramen Tavern

Located on Franklin st. Nojo provides that home feeling ramen that you miss from time to time. Coming from a Japan chain company, Nojo has very high standards for their ramen.

  • Their parent company owns established a chicken produce in Japan and has expanded ever since so you just know their produce do be high quality tho.

  • Although their price might be a little high, it's definitly worth it. thier soup is full of rich flavor and their noodles have the right texture.

  • We reccomened the Tonkatsu Ramen! A Definite must try.

Jijime

Jijme mainly focus on Korean and Japnese classics like ramen and Bimibap. Located on Geary between the bustle and hustle.

  • We reccomened the popcorn potstickers and the bulgogi plate. Delish!

  • You will always leave full, their meat to rice/ramen ratio is the perfect amount.

  • Price is a bit on the expensive side but you do get good food.

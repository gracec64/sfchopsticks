Boba Guys

Based from LA, Boba Guys is a pretty known staple in San Francisco. You can always expect long lines wrapped around their shops in the Mission and Union Square. When you walk into one their places you are greeted with a simple, clean and minimalistic enviornment.

  • Their drinks never have any artifical ingredidents. Their teas come from organic sources and milk from local farms.

  • They have new drinks year round and it's never the same. From theme drinks like 88rising to seasonal drinks like Mango Matcha

  • Also get their boba! Definitly one of the highest quality boba in the bay.

Tea Hut

Founded in 2017 in the Richmond district. It has become one of the best boba shops with their unique flavors. Similiar to Boba Guys they use natural ingredients and fresh fruit in their drinks.
  • They have more than just milk teas. Throughout their three years they have expanded into lattes and freezes.

  • Their drinks always come with a nice balance of tea flavor. Never underwheming or overpowering.

  • If you ever have the chance of going, get the Mango freeze!

Mr. & Mrs. Tea House

Also located in the Richmond district. We have Mr. and Mrs. Tea house. As a mom and pop boba shop they strive to provide the best boba for everyone.

  • They have become more enviroment friendly recently. Reducing plastic one cup at a time with sippable lids.

  • They provide a huge variety, with sago and snacks like popcorn chicken.

  • All their items come at a pretty affordable price compared to other boba shops on the market.

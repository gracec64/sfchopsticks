Poke Origin

Located in the inner Sunset disctrict. Poke Origin offers sushi grade quality at an affordable price point so anyone can enjoy.

  • They have a huge variety of diffrent kinds of poke you can choose from. 18 kinds and 20 toppings to be exact!

  • At a decennt you are able to get two scoops of fish mixed with sauce and toppings over rice.

  • We reccomended getting a medium as the small tends to not be filling.

I'a Poke

Right next to Mission dolorles park we have I'a Poke. They are also located in the design district and western addition.

  • Not only do they have themed bowls ready for you to choose but they also sushi burritos, aka sushiritto.

  • Everything they provide is a pretty good portions, their bowls and sushiritto are filling.

  • Although they are not cheap, thier food is worth it. The sauce and fried onions are to die for.

Poke Stop

Located right at the heart Mission district, we have a local poke shop called Poke Stop. Similiar to I'a Poke but with a twist. They also provide boba called Identitea. Score 2 in a 1!

  • Their preset bowls are mouth watering and delicous. They also have custom bowls if you prefer.

  • In terms of vaule, they go above and beyond. Unlimited toppings with no upcharge.

  • Close to transit and the bustling areas, come to get poke and explore the culture while you're at it.

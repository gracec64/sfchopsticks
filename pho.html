Kevin's Noodle House

If you've been to the bay then you have heard of Kevin's Noodle House. Over the years Kevin's Noodle House has expanded into a chain and quality has stayed the same.

  • They have a huge variety you can choose from. From all your Vietnamese classics to big rice plates that you love.

  • You can't beat their affordable prices. For just $10 you can get a large #1 at best quality.

  • Kevin's is the gift the gift that keeps on giving. With every bite of noodle you will always find beef for it.

Perilla

There are a couple places named Perilla around San Francisco, but the one I'm talking about is on Irving. They are pretty busy around lunch and dinner time with the typical waiting time of 30 minutes or less.

  • They are a neighborhood favorite and people rave about their garlic noodles and spicy chicken.

  • They really do care about their custmoers. They provide the best quality of the Viet food and always make sure you leave satisfed.

  • Their imperial rolls are to die for, try them out the next time you're in the area.

Turtle Tower

A classic in San Francisco with three locations in Civic Center, Richmond, and Soma. Thier speciality is the broth. Crafted from their family recipies from genorations before.

  • No two phos are alike. Their gift to the world is their range of flavors.

  • Their dine in setting is spacious and provides a lot of room for you to comfortably enjoy your food.

  • Locals rave about their chicken noodle soup and their chicken alone
